About Los Angeles

Located in Southern California on the coast of the Pacific Ocean, Los Angeles is the second most populous city in the United States and home to just under 4 million people. Known for being the center of the entertainment industry in the US, its beautiful beaches, and its year round warm weather, Los Angeles is a perfect travel destination with lots of unique places to visit and experiences to offer.
